User Charges

  • The applicants shall pay the user charges as prescribed below (through online payment only) and these charges are subject to revision by the Government from time to time. The user charges so collected shall be accrued in an escrow account, and shall be maintained by the State Level Nodal Agency and shall be used for meeting the expenses for implementation of TS-bPASS.
S.No. Plot area User charges
1 Up to 75 sq.yds Nil
2 Above 75 sq.yds and up 200 sq.mts Rs. 500
3 Above 200 sq.mts and up to 500 sq.mts Rs.1000
4 Above 500 sq.mts and up to 1000 sq.mts 1% of total fee
5 Above 1000 sq.mts and up to 2000 sq.mts 2% of total fee
6 Above 2000 sq.mts 2.5% of total fee
